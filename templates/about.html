About This Project

Purpose

This website provides daily liturgical resources for the Anglican Daily Office, following the lectionary and collects from The Book of Common Prayer (2019) of the Anglican Church in North America.

Why This Was Made

Rising early each morning to locate the readings, collects, and antiphons for Morning Prayer proved challenging, so this project grew out of a desire to simplify and streamline the process.

The goal was to create a simple, beautiful, and printable resource for daily prayer that follows the traditional Anglican patterns while being accessible on any device.

Coming Soon

Full 2026 Calendar: The complete liturgical year for 2026 (all 365 days) is being prepared and will be published once it is complete. Currently, the site contains a partial calendar as the data is being compiled and verified.

Attribution

All lectionary readings and collects are from The Book of Common Prayer (2019), Anglican Church in North America. This is an unofficial resource created for personal devotional use.
